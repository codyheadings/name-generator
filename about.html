About

so·bri·quet
/ˈsōbrəˌkā/
noun
a person's nickname.

Sobriquet is a project that I'm working on in my free time. The eventual goal is to have a sophisticated and customizable name generator experience with better tools for writers and artists than what is currently available. As a writer myself, I plan to make this site something that others like me would want to use.

Planned Features:

  • Ability to revert to previous generation
  • Save/store names
  • More categories
  • Toggleable style settings
  • Similar sound generation
  • THE SLOT MACHINE
